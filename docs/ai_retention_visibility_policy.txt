NON-AUTHORITATIVE REPOSITORY STATUS
- The AI repository is an intelligence archive and review archive.
- The AI repository is derived and non-authoritative for runtime truth.
- The AI repository is derived and non-authoritative for active plan truth.
- The AI repository is not a control surface.
- Repository storage of proposals, advisories, findings, or evidence does not grant direct control, runtime mutation, or plan mutation authority.

RETENTION CATEGORY TAXONOMY
- ROLLING_SHORT = current or recent rolling view with short horizon and supersedable entries.
- ROLLING_MEDIUM = bounded rolling history for medium-horizon review and incident context.
- REVIEW_CYCLE = retained across one formal review cycle or operator/admin review window.
- LONG_REVIEW_HISTORY = retained for longer institutional review and governance traceability.
- PROPOSAL_AUDIT_HISTORY = retained for proposal traceability, review, and later audit linkage.
- INCIDENT_FORENSIC_HISTORY = retained for incident forensics and later retrospective analysis.

RETENTION RULE FIELDS

VISIBILITY TIER INTENT
- INTERNAL_SHADOW_ONLY = internal shadow intelligence not surfaced as operator-facing by default.
- OPERATOR_VISIBLE = operator-visible derived intelligence.
- ADMIN_VISIBLE = admin-visible governance/intelligence outputs.
- REVIEW_BOARD_VISIBLE = review-board visibility for proposals, learning evidence, and deeper review artifacts.
- COMMITTEE_VISIBLE = high-level committee-facing summaries and review artifacts.

VISIBILITY ESCALATION RULES
- A record may be promoted from INTERNAL_SHADOW_ONLY to a higher tier only after explicit review in a later implementation phase.
- Promotion in visibility does not change authority_class or convert the record into runtime truth.
- REVIEWABLE_PROPOSAL_RECORD defaults to REVIEW_BOARD_VISIBLE and may be additionally ADMIN_VISIBLE.
- Shadow-only records may remain internal indefinitely if no review/escalation is justified.
- Council advisory records may be OPERATOR_VISIBLE but remain non-authoritative and non-directional.
- Executive briefs may be ADMIN_VISIBLE and COMMITTEE_VISIBLE without becoming control instructions.

MANIFEST POLICY
- Any repository manifest in A5 is a design/manifest placeholder only.
- A5 manifest files must be clearly labeled non-authoritative and pending future runtime repository implementation.
- Manifest existence does not mean runtime archive writing is implemented.

RELATIONSHIP TO DELIVERY LAYERS
- Future Telegram/email/admin delivery is deferred.
- Later delivery layers should read from repository-managed outputs, not ad hoc raw AI text.
- Repository-first external delivery later is the intended architecture boundary.

RELATIONSHIP TO LEARNING GOVERNANCE
- Repository retention and visibility support later L1-L7 learning-governance review traceability.
- Proposal records, evidence records, and review records are the main lineage surfaces for later governed learning discussions.
- Future post-change impact records may link to proposal/evidence lineage; this is deferred beyond A5.

ANTI-SPAM AND BOUNDED STORAGE INTENT
- Repository storage must remain bounded and structured.
- Duplicate incident outputs should not create uncontrolled duplicate repository entries.
- Recurrent hourly records should be rolling and compact.
- Council advisory history must remain compact and candidate-scoped.
- Retention intent must stay aligned with A4 cooldown/dedupe/suppression policy.
