purpose:
- define how runtime evidence may later feed edge scorecards conceptually
- keep runtime accounting separate from live control in this phase

what_runtime_accounting_means:
- runtime accounting means attributing observed decision and outcome evidence back to specific edge families and family-zone contexts over time.
- it answers not only whether a family trades, but how often it attempts, how often it is rejected or held, how often it executes, how it behaves by regime/session, and whether its health is improving or degrading.

conceptual_runtime_evidence_inputs:
- decision attempts
- candidate counts
- actual executions
- close outcomes
- reject reasons
- hold reasons
- execution-open failures
- regime-specific concentration
- session concentration
- family-specific deterioration patterns
- execution-quality drag or support
- geometry-sensitive degradation after attractive trigger appearance

accounting_windows:
- micro observation windows may later track attempt density and rapid degradation
- rolling windows may later track expectancy, mae, mfe, and concentration
- phase windows may later track deterioration and suppression pressure
- no window is activated in E8; only the model is defined

measurement_discipline:
- lack of executions is not automatically failure; it may reflect rarity or overblocking
- executions without quality are not evidence of edge health
- holds and rejects matter because they can reveal suppression pressure and overblocking risk
- regime/session concentration must remain visible because a family can look healthy overall while being narrowly dependent on one context
- execution quality interaction must remain visible because family health can be harmed by slippage, poor fill posture, or open failure patterns

non_runtime_boundary:
- runtime accounting in E8 does not compute live scores
- runtime accounting in E8 does not trigger suppression
- runtime accounting in E8 does not alter runtime journaling
- runtime accounting in E8 only defines the later accounting spine
