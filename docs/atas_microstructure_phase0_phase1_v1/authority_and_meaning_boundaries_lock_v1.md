# Authority and Meaning Boundaries Lock v1

## Program Scope Lock
- Program: `ATAS Microstructure Intelligence Engine Program`
- Implemented phase scope in this patch: `Phase 0 + Phase 1 only`
- Program posture: `RESEARCH / BUILD TRACK`
- Not in scope: operating-model replacement, live authority transfer, engine build phases.

## Formal Runtime Authority Lock
- MT5 remains the sole owner of runtime authority.
- MT5 remains the sole owner of decision authority.
- MT5 remains the sole owner of execution authority.
- MT5 remains the sole owner of risk and governor authority.
- MT5 remains the sole owner of final market meaning, final regime meaning, final canonical level meaning, and final tradability meaning.

## ATAS Role Lock
- ATAS is an external microstructure intelligence contributor only.
- ATAS outputs are non-authoritative and bounded.
- ATAS may provide context evidence, diagnostics, and interpretable hints.
- ATAS may not publish final decision packages or tradability verdicts.
- ATAS may not own final regime labels for MT5 runtime decisions.
- ATAS may not own canonical level meaning used as MT5 authority truth.

## Mandatory Separation Rules
- Semantic adapter remains mandatory between ATAS and MT5.
- Raw external feed must not enter MT5 runtime directly.
- Core packet and extended packet are separated by contract.
- Core packet can be considered for future bounded MT5 consumption candidate pathways.
- Extended packet remains optional, research-grade, non-binding, and non-operative for authority.

## Explicitly Allowed ATAS Build Scope
- Microstructure observation packaging.
- Cross-instrument basis evidence packaging (non-authoritative).
- Freshness/provenance/status/trace/suppression diagnostics.
- Optional interpretive hints under confidence ceiling and provenance requirements.
- Engine status and diagnostics artifacts.

## Explicitly Forbidden ATAS Build Scope
- Final regime ownership.
- Canonical level ownership.
- Final tradability ownership.
- Final decision packaging ownership.
- Execution/risk/governor control channels.
- MT5 runtime authority override semantics.

## AI and External Activation Lock
- No AI authority activation is introduced by this phase.
- AI readiness is data-shaping/observability only in this phase.
- Databento/Fusion activation changes are out of scope and locked out.

## Change Lock for Later Phases
- Any promotion of ATAS meaning scope beyond this lock requires explicit future phase approval and governance review.
