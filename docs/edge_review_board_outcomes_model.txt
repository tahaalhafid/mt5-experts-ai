EDGE REVIEW BOARD OUTCOMES MODEL — E11

Allowed review-board outcomes
- STRATEGIC_MAINTAIN
  - maintain current strategic posture without broadening scope
- STRATEGIC_FOCUS_RECOMMENDED
  - focus future attention on the clearest and structurally strongest families/areas
- STRATEGIC_TIGHTEN_SCOPE
  - reduce ambiguity by demanding cleaner family/zone boundaries or stronger conditions
- STRATEGIC_NARROW_SCOPE
  - reduce accepted scope of a family or context rather than treating it broadly
- STRATEGIC_DEPRIORITIZE
  - reduce strategic emphasis where fragility, weak evidence, or low-value loops dominate
- STRATEGIC_RESEARCH_MORE
  - acknowledge a real gap or unresolved area and return it to research/enrichment/intake pathways
- STRATEGIC_ESCALATE_FOR_LATER_ACTION_PREPARATION
  - signal that later governed preparation may be needed; not live action
- STRATEGIC_ARCHIVE_CONSIDERATION
  - signal that later archive consideration may be appropriate if no useful improvement appears

Outcome discipline
- Outcomes remain review-oriented only.
- Outcomes do not act as runtime council instructions.
- Outcomes do not grant direct promotion, suppression, or execution.
- Outcomes do not change licensing or runtime permissions in E11.

Review-record structure
Each review-board record should retain at minimum:
- review_record_id
- review_type
- reviewed_scope
- primary_inputs
- key_findings
- strategic_concern_note
- concentration_or_gap_note
- overlap_note
- fragility_note
- enrichment_value_note
- recommended_outcome
- confidence_posture
- lineage_reference
