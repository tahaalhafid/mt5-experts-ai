INTERNAL FACTORY SEMANTIC PROJECTION POLICY
version=PHASE4_INTERNAL_FACTORY_SEMANTIC_PROJECTION_POLICY_V1

semantic_projection purpose:
- project richer internal semantic understanding from gateway and decomposition inputs
- clarify structure without overwriting source meaning
- add analytical value only where a meaningful delta beyond decomposition exists
- keep unresolved ambiguity and limitations explicit

semantic_projection may add:
- semantic clarification
- projected factor linkage
- projected zone/regime refinement hypothesis
- projected role refinement hypothesis
- structural packaging posture

semantic_projection must not:
- restate decomposition with inflated confidence
- erase ambiguity dishonestly
- imply validation
- imply canon eligibility
- imply runtime permission

required projection areas:
- projected_market_thesis
- projected_setup_archetype
- projected_trigger_logic
- projected_timing_logic
- projected_invalidation_logic
- projected_risk_logic
- projected_exit_logic
- projected_confirmation_logic
- projected_context_logic
- projected_session_logic
- projected_volatility_logic
- projected_zone_fit_refinement
- projected_regime_fit_refinement
- projected_factory_role_refinement
- semantic_projection_confidence
- semantic_projection_limitations
