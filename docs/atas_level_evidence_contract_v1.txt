ATAS Level Evidence Contract v1 (Phase 0.5)

Purpose
- Export bounded level behavior evidence from ATAS into MT5 shadow context.
- Improve observability only.
- Do not create canonical level authority.

What ATAS May Export
- Candidate level observations (bounded max 3)
- For each candidate:
  - price and side candidate
  - level class and origin
  - session context
  - absorption/sweep/reclaim evidence
  - imbalance and divergence descriptors
  - local touch/reaction/stability properties
  - freshness/invalidation bounds
  - candidate role and market behavior tag

What ATAS May NOT Export
- Canonical final support
- Canonical final resistance
- Trade-ready level authority
- Entry/exit directives
- Risk sizing directives
- Governor directives

Authority Boundary
- Level behavior evidence: allowed as non-authoritative shadow context.
- Canonical support/resistance authority: MT5-only in Phase 0.5.
- Any ATAS level evidence is descriptive and bounded; never a decision command.

Consumption Boundary
- Consumption mode remains SHADOW_ONLY.
- No downstream live consumer may treat ATAS level candidates as execution-level truth.
