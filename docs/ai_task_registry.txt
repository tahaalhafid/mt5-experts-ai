GLOBAL CONSTRAINTS
- AI_OFF permits no task execution.
- AI_SHADOW_ONLY permits shadow/internal/review-facing outputs only.
- AI_ADVISORY_ONLY may additionally permit REVIEWABLE_PROPOSALS via BOUNDED_PROPOSAL_GENERATION.
- No task family may issue direct trading commands.
- No task family may auto-apply changes.
- No task family may mutate strategy, governor, execution, thresholds, or live runtime behavior.
- COUNCIL_CONTEXTUAL_ADVISORY remains contextual and non-directional only.
