EDGE INVALIDATION STATE MODEL
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory invalidation-state model only.
Truth posture: Invalidation-state interpretation only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
INVALIDATION STATES
============================================================
- INVALIDATION_NOT_APPLICABLE
  Under suppressive or non-licensable posture, invalidation is intentionally not interpreted for factory progression.

- INVALIDATION_NOT_REACHED
  The thesis has not yet structurally failed.

- INVALIDATION_EARLY_WARNING
  Warning signs are emerging, but the thesis is not yet conclusively broken.

- INVALIDATION_PENDING
  Breakdown risk is meaningful and the thesis is near a real failure threshold.

- INVALIDATION_CONFIRMED
  Structural thesis failure is clear enough that the factory should treat the prior edge logic as broken.

- INVALIDATION_STRUCTURAL
  The broader structure supporting the thesis materially failed.

- INVALIDATION_RECLAIM_FAILURE
  Reclaim-based logic materially failed or was lost.

- INVALIDATION_ACCEPTANCE_FAILURE
  Acceptance beyond break/reclaim/release did not hold sufficiently to preserve the thesis.

- INVALIDATION_CONTINUATION_FAILURE
  Continuation failed to resume or structurally hold.

- INVALIDATION_EXHAUSTION_COLLAPSE
  The thesis reached a spent/exhausted state and then collapsed against the expected continuation or reversal logic.

============================================================
STATE DISCIPLINE
============================================================
- INVALIDATION_EARLY_WARNING is not INVALIDATION_CONFIRMED.
- INVALIDATION_PENDING is not yet full thesis failure.
- INVALIDATION_CONFIRMED is not a runtime stop instruction.
- INVALIDATION_NOT_APPLICABLE should dominate when regime posture is suppressive.
- The factory must preserve ambiguity when it cannot honestly separate noise from thesis failure.

============================================================
AMBIGUITY DISCIPLINE
============================================================
Use ambiguity conservatively when:
- the trigger itself was still incomplete
- the thesis boundary was broad or under-specified
- the difference between reclaim failure and ordinary probe was still weak
- continuation deterioration was visible but not yet structurally decisive
- micro-release failure is too noisy to classify cleanly
