health_postures:
- HEALTH_STRONG
- HEALTH_STABLE
- HEALTH_MIXED
- HEALTH_FRAGILE
- HEALTH_UNPROVEN

health_posture_meaning:
- HEALTH_STRONG is reserved for later evidence-rich families and is not widely claimed in E8
- HEALTH_STABLE means the family/context is structurally mature enough to justify future accounting without making performance claims
- HEALTH_MIXED means the family/context is structurally relevant but carries meaningful overlap, secondary-role, or context-fragility concerns
- HEALTH_FRAGILE means ambiguity, crowding, noisy confirmation, or geometry sensitivity are already visible
- HEALTH_UNPROVEN means the family/context remains too lightly supported for confident health claims

deterioration_postures:
- DETERIORATION_NONE_VISIBLE
- DETERIORATION_EARLY_SIGNAL
- DETERIORATION_REPEAT_SIGNAL
- DETERIORATION_MATERIAL
- DETERIORATION_UNKNOWN

deterioration_meaning:
- deterioration requires evidence over time; it should not be fabricated from one structural impression
- DETERIORATION_UNKNOWN is the conservative default when runtime accounting feed is not yet active
- later deterioration signals should consider expectancy drift, worsening mae/mfe posture, rising hold/reject pressure, session/regime concentration, and repeated geometry failure

conservative_truth_rule:
- no strong health claim from architecture alone
- no deterioration claim from narrative alone
- deterioration visibility should stay separate from simple dislike or stylistic preference
- a family may remain structurally elegant while its future accounting health turns fragile
- a family may remain structurally narrow without being invalid; accounting must later decide whether rarity, suppression, or deterioration is the real cause

later_governance_note:
- health and deterioration postures defined here prepare later promotion/suppression governance only.
