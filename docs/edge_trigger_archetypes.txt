Authority: Institutional Edge Factory trigger archetype design only.
Truth posture: Trigger archetype definitions only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
TRIGGER ARCHETYPES
============================================================
- SWEEP_THEN_RECLAIM
  A liquidity sweep or raid is followed by reclaim/acceptance behavior that turns the sweep into a timing event.

- BREAK_THEN_ACCEPT
  A structural break occurs, then price shows acceptance beyond the break rather than only momentary excursion.

- BREAK_THEN_RETEST_ACCEPT
  A break is followed by retest and renewed acceptance. This archetype requires more patience than immediate breakout chase logic.

- PULLBACK_HOLD_THEN_EXPAND
  Trend structure holds during pullback, then re-expands in the trend direction.

- FAILED_BREAK_RETURN
  A break attempt fails and price returns into the prior structure with sufficient rejection/acceptance quality.

- RANGE_EDGE_REJECTION
  A range edge interaction rejects and rotates away from the edge with enough quality to matter.

- COMPRESSION_RELEASE
  Compressed structure releases into directional expansion.

- MICRO_EXPANSION_RELEASE
  Small local structure releases into micro expansion; highly late-sensitive and easily degraded by noise.

- MOMENTUM_CONTINUATION_CONFIRM
  Continuation thesis requires visible continuation confirmation via expansion/close/follow-through quality.

- POST_DISPLACEMENT_REENTRY
  Displacement already occurred; timing becomes valid when the reentry/reload condition preserves initiative quality.
