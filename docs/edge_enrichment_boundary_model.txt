1) FORBIDDEN LOOP PATHS
- RUNTIME_EVIDENCE_TO_DIRECT_RUNTIME_MUTATION
- SCORECARD_TO_DIRECT_SUPPRESSION
- AI_OUTPUT_TO_DIRECT_PROMOTION
- ENRICHMENT_TO_RUNTIME_BYPASS
- GOVERNANCE_SIGNAL_TO_LIVE_PARAMETER_MUTATION
- LOOP_OUTPUT_TO_AUTHORITY_ELEVATION

2) WHY THESE PATHS ARE FORBIDDEN
They would collapse the factory into uncontrolled self-modification, bypass review, or expand authority beyond the bounded design already established.

3) BOUNDARY NOTE
E10 enrichment may register, route, rescope, re-intake, decompose, review, research, or archive.
It may NOT mutate runtime, override governance, or create direct trading control.

4) HONESTY RULE
If an enrichment signal is weak, repetitive, vague, or blocked by governance boundary, it must remain visibly weak, repetitive, vague, or blocked.
It must not be relabeled as progress by narrative alone.
