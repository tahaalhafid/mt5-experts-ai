SUFFICIENCY STATES
- THIN_EVIDENCE
  description=Evidence is too weak, narrow, ambiguous, stale, or poorly linked for meaningful learning conclusions.
  usage_limit=May be archived or used to justify further collection, but not strong proposal support.
  escalation_note=Thin evidence should be labeled honestly and not upgraded by narrative.

- MINIMAL_EVIDENCE
  description=Evidence supports preliminary discussion but remains narrow, weakly corroborated, or limited in scope.
  usage_limit=May support bounded review framing or request-for-more-evidence.
  escalation_note=Strong claims remain inappropriate.

- SUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE
  description=Evidence is materially usable for a bounded claim and is adequately corroborated for governed proposal or review support.
  usage_limit=May support proposal and learning review discussions.
  escalation_note=Still requires governed review and does not authorize mutation.

- STRONG_CORROBORATED_EVIDENCE
  description=Evidence is well-linked, adequately broad for its claim, corroborated across strong or complementary families, and limitations are disclosed.
  usage_limit=May support stronger proposal or experiment-candidate review.
  escalation_note=Even strong evidence remains non-mutative and review-governed.

QUALITY GRADES
- LOW_QUALITY
  meaning=Evidence is weakly linked, narrow, stale, ambiguous, noisy, or poorly corroborated.
  expected_handling=Archive, monitor, or request more evidence.

- MODERATE_QUALITY
  meaning=Evidence is usable with disclosed limitations and bounded claims, but still has notable gaps or modest corroboration.
  expected_handling=Use cautiously for bounded review and proposal support.

- HIGH_QUALITY
  meaning=Evidence is well-linked, sufficiently recent for its claim, scope-clear, corroborated, and supported by review context where relevant.
  expected_handling=Eligible for stronger review attention and experiment-candidate support.

QUALITY DRIVERS
Positive drivers:
- sample_size_adequacy_for_claim
- recency_fit_for_claim
- linkage_quality
- source_reliability
- corroboration_breadth
- scope_clarity
- review_support_status
- duplicate_noise_control

Negative drivers:
- stale_data
- weak_linkage
- unresolved_ambiguity
- narrow_scope_with_strong_claim
- duplicated_or_repetitive_noise
- low_review_confidence
- partial_or_missing_context
- regime_or_scope_mismatch

QUALITY DISCIPLINE RULES
- Strong narrative style must not raise quality grade by itself.
- Ambiguity must reduce quality or keep evidence at a lower sufficiency state.
- Sparse evidence may still be high-quality for a narrow claim, but not for a broad claim.
- Repeated noisy evidence does not become high-quality merely by repetition.
- Review support increases confidence only when the rationale is explicit and linked.

EVIDENCE BUNDLE TYPES

- LEARNING_EVIDENCE_BUNDLE
  required_evidence_families=At least 1 primary family plus 1 corroborating or contextual family unless explicit thin/minimal status is disclosed
  optional_evidence_families=Any bounded supporting family including advisory effectiveness, review outcomes, proposal history, or operator feedback
  minimum_sufficiency_threshold=MINIMAL_EVIDENCE for preliminary governed review; SUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE preferred
  preferred_corroboration_pattern=diagnostic/replay/validation plus review or history linkage
  required_limitation_disclosure=Yes
  review_relevance=General learning review and governed understanding

- PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  required_evidence_families=At least 1 strong primary family plus corroboration from review-governed, validation, diagnostic, replay, or history evidence
  optional_evidence_families=advisory effectiveness, root cause, anomaly, operator feedback as supporting context
  minimum_sufficiency_threshold=SUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE
  preferred_corroboration_pattern=runtime-linked or validation-linked evidence plus review outcome or history support
  required_limitation_disclosure=Yes
  review_relevance=Proposal review and bounded implementation planning only

- EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  required_evidence_families=At least 2 corroborating families with at least 1 strong primary family and explicit limitation disclosure
  optional_evidence_families=effectiveness review outputs, proposal history, learning history, governance audit context
  minimum_sufficiency_threshold=STRONG_CORROBORATED_EVIDENCE preferred; SUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE only for tightly bounded experiment candidates with explicit caution
  preferred_corroboration_pattern=validation/replay/diagnostic evidence plus review-governed outcomes and impact-oriented context
  required_limitation_disclosure=Yes
  review_relevance=Review-board experiment consideration only

BUNDLE COMPOSITION RESTRICTIONS
- Bundles may not hide unresolved contradictions.
- Bundles may not omit thin-evidence labeling where applicable.
- Bundles may not turn contextual/operator feedback into primary authority.
- Bundles may not become a direct control path, mutation path, or plan-overwrite path.
- Bundles must preserve source hierarchy and sufficiency disclosure.

AMBIGUITY AND MIXED-SIGNAL DISCIPLINE
- Thin evidence must be labeled as THIN_EVIDENCE.
- Ambiguity must be surfaced explicitly.
- Unresolved linkage issues reduce quality.
- Insufficient evidence remains insufficient even if the narrative is sophisticated.
- Mixed signals must be recorded honestly rather than collapsed into false certainty.

TRACEABILITY EXPECTATIONS
Each governed evidence bundle should conceptually track:
- evidence_bundle_id
- evidence_family_members
- source_artifact_refs
- source_time_window
- scope_label
- affected_zone_or_family
- sufficiency_state
- quality_grade
- corroboration_note
- limitations_note
- downstream_review_relevance

AI ROLE IN THE EVIDENCE PIPELINE
AI may:
- assemble evidence
- analyze evidence
- classify evidence
- summarize evidence
- package bounded evidence bundles

AI may not:
- bypass sufficiency rules
- promote weak evidence to strong evidence by narrative alone
- convert evidence bundles into direct runtime mutation
- override source hierarchy by verbosity, confidence styling, or formatting

FINAL RULE
Evidence quality and bundle strength support governance decisions only.
They do not authorize direct live changes, direct learning mutation, or authority elevation.
