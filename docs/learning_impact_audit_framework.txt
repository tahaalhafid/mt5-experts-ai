PURPOSE
Define how governed learning outcomes are audited for real impact under prior H/A and L1-L6 constraints.
This framework judges impact by measured or reviewable outcome, not by implementation effort, narrative sophistication, or procedural momentum.

CORE INTERPRETATION RULES
- learning impact must be judged conservatively
- no strong success claim from thin evidence windows
- no success claim based solely on approval counts
- no success claim based solely on promotion readiness
- no success claim based solely on narrative clarity or polished packaging
- governance compliance and boundedness matter as much as apparent insight
- review activity alone does not prove usefulness
- side-effects and burden must be reviewed alongside upside

IMPACT_INTERPRETATION_STATES
- POSITIVE_IMPACT
  purpose=governed learning outputs improved usefulness, review quality, bounded experiment readiness, or future planning discipline without material burden, boundary pressure, or rollback concern
- MIXED_IMPACT
  purpose=learning outputs showed some useful value but also introduced meaningful noise, burden, ambiguity, or side-effect concern
- INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE
  purpose=evidence windows are too thin, traceability is incomplete, or impact claims remain premature
- NEGATIVE_IMPACT
  purpose=learning outputs created weak proposals, boundary pressure, burden, noise, repeated disqualification, rollback pressure, or misleading confidence

OPTIONAL_RATING_BANDS
- LOW
- MODERATE
- HIGH

AUDIT_WINDOWS
- DAILY_OBSERVATION
  what_may_be_reviewed=early output quality, traceability completeness, immediate burden/noise signals
  acceptable_conclusions=light observations only
  premature_conclusions=strong trust increase, strong success claims, strong scope expansion claims
  thin_evidence_condition=default high
- THREE_DAY_AUDIT
  what_may_be_reviewed=early pattern consistency, review usefulness, repeated disqualification, boundedness discipline
  acceptable_conclusions=preliminary mixed/insufficient/early positive or negative patterns
  premature_conclusions=strong long-horizon success claims
  thin_evidence_condition=still material
- WEEKLY_AUDIT
  what_may_be_reviewed=multi-cycle review value, experiment-design readiness quality, repeated side-effects, burden trends
  acceptable_conclusions=moderate governance conclusions when traceability is complete
  premature_conclusions=permanent trust inflation or broad scope expansion
  thin_evidence_condition=depends on volume and linkage quality
- PHASE_AUDIT
  what_may_be_reviewed=full governed learning cycle impact across proposal, review, experimentation-design, promotion-readiness, and rollback discipline
  acceptable_conclusions=best available governed impact judgment under bounded evidence
  premature_conclusions=runtime mutation authorization or auto-promotion
  thin_evidence_condition=applies if phase volume or traceability remains weak

IMPACT_DIMENSIONS
- LEARNING_USEFULNESS
  impact_purpose=judge whether governed learning improved understanding, structured reasoning, or bounded next-step readiness
  typical_evidence_sources=learning proposals, review outcomes, impact notes, repository history
  review_audience=ADMIN_REVIEWER|REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=PRIMARY
- PROPOSAL_USEFULNESS
  impact_purpose=judge whether learning proposals were relevant, bounded, review-worthy, and not procedural noise
  typical_evidence_sources=L3 proposals, L4 decisions, proposal history
  review_audience=ADMIN_REVIEWER|REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=PRIMARY
- EXPERIMENT_USEFULNESS
  impact_purpose=judge whether experiment designs improved validation readiness and bounded decision quality without hidden rollout pressure
  typical_evidence_sources=L5 experiment designs, validation notes, rollback readiness notes
  review_audience=REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=PRIMARY
- REVIEW_VALUE
  impact_purpose=judge whether learning artifacts improved reviewer clarity, prioritization, or decision quality
  typical_evidence_sources=review decisions, require-more-evidence patterns, closure notes
  review_audience=ADMIN_REVIEWER|REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- RISK_REDUCTION_OR_RISK_INCREASE
  impact_purpose=judge whether governed learning reduced guesswork and unmanaged risk, or created new risk pressure
  typical_evidence_sources=promotion reviews, rollback notes, governance notes
  review_audience=REVIEW_BOARD|COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- NOISE_IMPACT
  impact_purpose=judge whether learning created low-value proposal inflation, repetitive packaging, or weak-evidence traffic
  typical_evidence_sources=proposal volume, closure patterns, repeated disqualification
  review_audience=ADMIN_REVIEWER|REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- GOVERNANCE_BURDEN
  impact_purpose=judge review burden, escalation pressure, and governance overhead introduced by learning
  typical_evidence_sources=review queue history, escalation history, closure patterns
  review_audience=ADMIN_REVIEWER|REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- SIDE_EFFECT_EMERGENCE
  impact_purpose=judge unintended consequences such as review fatigue, scope pressure, or hidden boundary stress
  typical_evidence_sources=side-effect notes, burden notes, disqualification patterns
  review_audience=REVIEW_BOARD|COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- ROLLBACK_PRESSURE
  impact_purpose=judge whether learning outputs increase concern about future rollback needs or insufficient rollback preparation
  typical_evidence_sources=rollback discipline notes, promotion reviews, disqualification reasons
  review_audience=REVIEW_BOARD
  audit_relevance=HIGH
- LEARNING_SCOPE_FIT
  impact_purpose=judge whether learning remains within appropriate bounded scope rather than pressing for unjustified expansion
  typical_evidence_sources=scope proposals, trust adjustment notes, governance review
  review_audience=REVIEW_BOARD|COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  audit_relevance=HIGH

FORMAL_SIDE_EFFECT_REVIEW_POLICY
The following must be audited explicitly:
- governance burden increase
- review fatigue
- proposal inflation
- weak evidence packaging
- rollback pressure increase
- scope drift pressure
- repeated disqualification patterns
- narrative sophistication masking weak value
- hidden boundary pressure

Explicit rule:
well_written_learning_outputs_are_not_automatically_high_value_learning_outputs=true
