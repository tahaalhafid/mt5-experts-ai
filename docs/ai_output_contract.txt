GLOBAL OUTPUT RULES
- All AI outputs in this project are derived, bounded, reviewable artifacts.
- No AI output may claim runtime-truth authority.
- No AI output may claim plan-truth authority.
- No AI output may contain direct BUY/SELL commands.
- No AI output may contain direct execution instructions.
- No AI output may contain direct plan-mutation commands.
- No AI output may contain direct threshold/governor mutation commands for auto-apply.
- No AI output may contain hidden veto language outside the allowed council advisory packet schema.
- Proposal-like outputs are reviewable only and never directly applicable from output contract alone.

PROHIBITED OUTPUT CONTENT
- direct BUY/SELL commands
- direct execution orders
- direct plan mutation commands
- direct threshold mutation instructions for auto-apply
- governor override commands
- hidden veto language outside allowed council advisory packet schema
- claims of runtime truth authority
- claims of plan truth authority
- implied auto-apply language
- hidden direct-control wording

OUTPUT BOUNDARY RULES
- SHADOW outputs may be generated only as derived/internal/review artifacts.
- ADVISORY outputs may be visible to operator/admin/review flows but remain non-authoritative.
- Proposal outputs are reviewable only.
- Council advisory outputs are non-directional only.
- Learning governance outputs are evidence/proposal only.
