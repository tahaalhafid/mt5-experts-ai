FORMAL DECISION OUTCOMES

- REJECT
  outcome_purpose=Stop the current proposal from progressing because its evidence, boundedness, traceability, risk framing, or mutation discipline is not acceptable.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=No governed next step proceeds from the current proposal package.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It does not erase history and it does not imply that future stronger proposals are forbidden.
  allowed_next_steps=ARCHIVE_AND_CLOSE,OPTIONAL_FUTURE_NEW_PROPOSAL_UNDER_NEW_EVIDENCE

- DEFER
  outcome_purpose=Pause a bounded proposal that may still be relevant later when timing, readiness, or priority changes.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=The proposal is not progressing now, but is not necessarily invalid in principle.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It does not mean silent approval and it does not keep the proposal open forever without re-entry conditions.
  allowed_next_steps=WAIT_FOR_REENTRY_CONDITION,REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE,LATER_REVIEW,CLOSE_NO_ACTION

- REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE
  outcome_purpose=Force stronger, clearer, or better-linked support before the proposal may be reconsidered.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=Current evidence is not sufficient for the proposal's claimed scope or risk.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It does not imply likely future acceptance and it does not let narrative confidence substitute for missing evidence.
  allowed_next_steps=EVIDENCE_STRENGTHENING,REPACKAGING,LATER_REVIEW,REJECT

- ACCEPT_FOR_EXPERIMENT
  outcome_purpose=Mark a proposal as eligible for later controlled experimentation consideration in a future phase.
  who_may_issue_it=REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=The proposal is sufficiently bounded and supported to enter a later governed experimentation consideration path.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It is not runtime activation, not experiment launch, not plan overwrite, not threshold mutation, and not auto-apply.
  allowed_next_steps=NON_MUTATIVE_HANDOFF_TO_L5_OR_LATER_EXPERIMENT_GOVERNANCE,ARCHIVE_DECISION_RECORD

- ACCEPT_FOR_GOVERNED_ACTION_PREPARATION
  outcome_purpose=Mark a proposal as eligible for later planning, preparation, or structured governance continuation.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=The proposal may move into later governed planning or preparation work only.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It is not live activation, not implementation, not runtime control, not parameter mutation, and not permission to bypass later review.
  allowed_next_steps=NON_MUTATIVE_HANDOFF_TO_LATER_PLANNING_OR_GOVERNED_PREPARATION,ARCHIVE_DECISION_RECORD

- ESCALATE
  outcome_purpose=Transfer the proposal to a higher or more appropriate reviewer role with explicit reason.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD
  what_it_means=The current reviewer will not finalize the proposal and a more appropriate reviewer must handle it.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It does not imply probable acceptance and it does not automatically strengthen weak evidence.
  allowed_next_steps=UNDER_HIGHER_REVIEW,REJECT,DEFER,REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE,ACCEPT_OUTCOME

- CLOSE_NO_ACTION
  outcome_purpose=End the current review pass with no governed next step under the current scope or window.
  who_may_issue_it=ADMIN_REVIEWER,REVIEW_BOARD,COMMITTEE_REVIEWER
  what_it_means=The proposal is archived without current continuation.
  what_it_does_not_mean=It does not imply that history is discarded or that future better-bounded proposals are impossible.
  allowed_next_steps=ARCHIVE_AND_CLOSE,OPTIONAL_FUTURE_NEW_PROPOSAL_UNDER_NEW_SCOPE_OR_EVIDENCE

EXPLICIT ACCEPTANCE MEANING
- ACCEPT_FOR_EXPERIMENT = eligibility for later governed experimentation consideration only.
- ACCEPT_FOR_GOVERNED_ACTION_PREPARATION = eligibility for later planning/preparation/review continuation only.
- acceptance_is_not_runtime_activation=true
- acceptance_is_not_plan_overwrite=true
- acceptance_is_not_threshold_mutation=true
- acceptance_is_not_strategy_change=true
- acceptance_is_not_governor_change=true
- acceptance_is_not_execution_change=true
- acceptance_is_not_auto_apply=true

DEFER REJECT ESCALATION AND CLOSURE DISCIPLINE
- defer_if=timing_priority_or_context_is_not_yet_right_but_boundedness_is_sound
- reject_if=mutation_path_is_hidden,evidence_is_too_weak,traceability_is_missing,boundedness_is_missing,risk_framing_is_unacceptable
- require_more_evidence_if=proposal_is_plausible_but_support_is_not_sufficient_for_scope_or_risk
- escalate_if=risk_or_governance_scope_exceeds_current_reviewer_or_cross_scope_conflict_exists
- close_if=proposal_is_rejected,archived_after_handoff,explicitly_no_actioned,or_superseded
- endless_defer_loops_forbidden=true

AI ROLE BOUNDARY
- AI may help clarify proposal rationale for reviewers.
- AI may summarize evidence and limitations.
- AI may not issue decision outcomes.
- AI may not self-approve or self-escalate into approval.
- AI may not convert an accepted outcome into runtime change.
