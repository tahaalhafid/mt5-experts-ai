scope=LEARNING_TRUST_AND_TIGHTENING_GOVERNANCE_ONLY
runtime_mutation_authorized=false
auto_adjustment_allowed=false

PURPOSE
Define conservative, non-mutative trust and tightening outcomes informed by learning impact audit.

ALLOWED_GOVERNANCE_OUTCOMES
- CONTINUE_LEARNING_AS_IS
  meaning=current governed learning scope may continue under existing bounded controls
  does_not_mean=runtime_change_or_scope_expansion
- CONTINUE_WITH_TIGHTER_EVIDENCE_REQUIREMENTS
  meaning=raise evidence and corroboration expectations before future proposals or promotion readiness
  does_not_mean=automatic_runtime_restriction_or_apply
- CONTINUE_WITH_NARROWER_SCOPE
  meaning=limit future learning to tighter domains, families, or bounded contexts
  does_not_mean=runtime_mutation
- REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE_BEFORE_PROMOTION
  meaning=future promotion-readiness consideration must wait for stronger support
  does_not_mean=rejection_of_all_learning
- DOWNGRADE_PROMOTION_READINESS_CONFIDENCE
  meaning=reduce confidence in current readiness claims until stronger validation or traceability exists
  does_not_mean=runtime_rollback_execution
- HOLD_LEARNING_SCOPE_EXPANSION
  meaning=do not expand learning scope based on current impact record
  does_not_mean=disable_current_governed_scope_automatically
- RECOMMEND_STRONGER_BOUNDING
  meaning=require tighter proposal, experiment-design, and promotion-readiness framing
  does_not_mean=immediate_process_rewrite
- RECOMMEND_ROLLBACK_PREPAREDNESS_STRENGTHENING
  meaning=require stronger rollback readiness expectations for future promotion consideration
  does_not_mean=rollback_execution_or_live_change

SUCCESS_AND_FAILURE_INTERPRETATION
- POSITIVE_IMPACT
  conservative_rule=only when governed learning improves review quality, proposal usefulness, or experiment readiness without material burden, repeated disqualification, boundary pressure, or rollback concern
- MIXED_IMPACT
  conservative_rule=when useful structure exists but burden, noise, ambiguity, or side-effects remain significant
- INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE
  conservative_rule=when traceability is weak, windows are thin, or conclusions remain premature
- NEGATIVE_IMPACT
  conservative_rule=when learning outputs repeatedly create weak proposals, excess burden, hidden pressure, or governance contradiction

STRICT_CONSERVATIVE_RULES
- no_strong_success_claim_from_thin_windows=true
- no_success_claim_from_approval_counts_alone=true
- no_success_claim_from_narrative_clarity_alone=true
- promotion_readiness_alone_does_not_prove_impact=true
- review_activity_alone_does_not_prove_usefulness=true
- boundedness_and_governance_compliance_are_load_bearing=true

FORMAL_SIDE_EFFECT_REVIEW_REQUIREMENTS
Audit must explicitly look for:
- governance burden increase
- review fatigue
- proposal inflation
- weak evidence packaging
- rollback pressure increase
- scope drift pressure
- repeated disqualification patterns
- narrative sophistication masking weak value
- hidden boundary pressure

Explicit policy line:
well_written_learning_outputs_are_not_automatically_high_value=true

AI_ROLE_BOUNDARY
AI may:
- summarize impact evidence
- summarize burden and limitations
- package bounded impact-review inputs

AI may NOT:
- self_certify_learning_success
- self_adjust_trust
- self_narrow_or_self_expand_scope
- convert_impact_audit_into_live_change
- bypass_human_governance

TRACEABILITY_EXPECTATIONS
A trust-adjustment recommendation should conceptually capture:
- learning_impact_audit_record_id
- impact_dimensions_evaluated
- impact_signal
- impact_limitations
- trust_adjustment_recommendation
- recommendation_rationale
- linked_repository_records
- reviewer_role
- audit_timestamp

RELATIONSHIP_TO_LATER_ACTION
- L7 informs future learning-scope governance
- L7 may justify tighter requirements or narrower scope
- L7 may justify stronger rollback-preparedness expectations
- L7 does NOT activate runtime changes
- L7 does NOT replace implementation governance
- L7 does NOT authorize live mutation or authority expansion
