EDGE ENRICHMENT REENTRY RULES
phase_id=E10
model_role=ALLOWED_REENTRY_PATH_DISCIPLINE

1) ALLOWED REENTRY PATHS
- RAW_SOURCE_TO_INTAKE_REENTRY
- MARKET_NATIVE_FINDING_TO_DECOMPOSITION_REENTRY
- SCORECARD_SIGNAL_TO_GOVERNANCE_REVIEW
- DETERIORATION_SIGNAL_TO_NARROWING_REVIEW
- SUPPRESSION_SIGNAL_TO_RESEARCH_OR_ARCHIVE_REENTRY
- GOVERNANCE_OUTCOME_TO_RESCOPING_REENTRY
- INTERNAL_RUNTIME_FINDING_TO_ENRICHMENT_REENTRY

2) STATUS MODEL
- ENRICHMENT_SIGNAL_REGISTERED
- ENRICHMENT_REENTRY_ELIGIBLE
- ENRICHMENT_RESEARCH_ONLY
- ENRICHMENT_REVIEW_REQUIRED
- ENRICHMENT_BLOCKED_BY_BOUNDARY
- ENRICHMENT_READY_FOR_RESCOPING
- ENRICHMENT_READY_FOR_REINTAKE

3) REENTRY DISCIPLINE
- scorecard signals may justify governance review, not direct promotion/suppression
- deterioration signals may justify narrowing review, not direct live disabling
- suppression signals may justify research/archive re-entry, not direct runtime removal
- governance outcomes may justify rescoping re-entry, not direct implementation
- new raw sources must re-enter through intake
- new market-native findings must re-enter through decomposition

4) RECORD FIELDS
Each enrichment record should preserve:
- enrichment_record_id
- input_origin
- input_type
- source_reference
- target_reentry_path
- enrichment_reason
- related_material_ids
- related_zone_or_regime
- related_scorecard_or_governance_refs
- allowed_next_stage
- forbidden_next_stage_note
- confidence_posture
- lineage_reference
