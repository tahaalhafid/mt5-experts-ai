GLOBAL TRIGGER RULES
- All tasks remain subject to H6 authority state.
- All tasks remain summary-first by default.
- Deep investigation is permitted only where the escalation policy allows it.
- Duplicate incident suppression should prefer decision_id, position_id, deal_id, incident window, or review window scoping.
- Cooldown intent must respect H6 guardrails:
  AIHourlyRunCap, AIDeepInvestigationDailyCap, AITriggerCooldownMinutes.
