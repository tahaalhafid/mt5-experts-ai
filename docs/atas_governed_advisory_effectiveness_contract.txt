ATAS Governed Advisory Effectiveness Contract (Level 3)
Version: 1
Language: English only

Purpose
- Provide bounded, auditable observability for advisory behavior.
- Support governance review without creating execution authority.

What is measured
- advisory_total
- advisory_ok_total
- advisory_caution_total
- advisory_strong_caution_total
- advisory_insufficient_evidence_total
- advisory_hold_total
- advisory_flag_total
- advisory_display_only_total
- advisory_semantic_only_total
- advisory_translation_applied_total
- advisory_translation_suppressed_total
- advisory_contradiction_total
- advisory_confirmation_total

What is not measured here
- trade execution authority outcomes
- direct risk envelope control
- governor decisions
- AI authority changes

Interpretation boundary
- Effectiveness counters are evidence-only derived surfaces.
- They must not be consumed as execution authority.
- They must not auto-enable stronger rollout behavior.

Review use
- Governance can use counters to evaluate advisory utility and safety.
- Any rollout escalation must remain explicit and controlled.
