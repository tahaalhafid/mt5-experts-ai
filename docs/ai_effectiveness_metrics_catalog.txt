METRIC STATUS RULES
- metric_status=ACTIVE_WHEN_RUNTIME_DATA_EXISTS means the metric is legitimate but depends on actual repository/runtime evidence.
- metric_status=PLACEHOLDER_PENDING_RUNTIME_MEASUREMENT means the metric is defined for governance review but not yet reliably measurable from current package state.
- metric_status=QUALITATIVE_REVIEW_REQUIRED means the metric must be assessed through review notes rather than forced numeric precision.
- Do not fabricate numeric certainty when evidence is missing, shallow, or ambiguous.

METRIC INTERPRETATION DISCIPLINE
- No single metric should dominate effectiveness conclusions when evidence is thin.
- Advisory benefit metrics must be balanced against blocked-win penalty and false-caution risk.
- Proposal acceptance counts alone do not prove proposal quality or runtime value.
- Review burden and cost can outweigh apparent analytical richness.
- Governance breach metrics have high seriousness even when counts are low.
