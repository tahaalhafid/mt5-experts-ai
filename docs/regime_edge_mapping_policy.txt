REGIME-EDGE MAPPING POLICY
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory regime-to-edge mapping policy only.
Truth posture: Factory structural compatibility mapping only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
PHASE IDENTITY
============================================================
Phase:
- E4 — Regime-to-Edge Mapping Engine

Purpose:
- Define the formal regime-to-edge mapping layer inside the factory.
- Specify which zones and families are structurally compatible with which market regimes.
- Distinguish regime compatibility from zone membership and from runtime permissions.

Non-goals:
- No runtime activation
- No trigger approval
- No execution permission
- No runtime export
- No code behavior change

============================================================
WHAT REGIME-TO-EDGE MAPPING MEANS
============================================================
Regime-to-edge mapping means:
- the factory assigns structural compatibility posture between regimes and edge zones/families
- regime mapping decides whether a zone or family is structurally appropriate, secondary, conditional, weak-fit, or suppressed under a regime
- regime mapping is stricter than zone membership alone

Regime-to-edge mapping does NOT mean:
- runtime licensing
- execution permission
- trigger approval
- guaranteed profitability
- automatic canon promotion
- automatic suppression in current runtime code

============================================================
CANONICAL REGIMES
============================================================
The formal canonical regimes in E4 are:
- TREND_UP
- TREND_DOWN
- RANGE_BALANCED
- RANGE_DIRTY
- COMPRESSION
- EXPANSION
- TRANSITION
- NO_TRADE
- UNKNOWN

These regime names remain compact on purpose.
E4 does not inflate regime count beyond what the current factory can support meaningfully.

============================================================
MAPPING PRINCIPLES
============================================================
1) Zone membership does not guarantee regime approval.
2) A family may be strong in one regime and suppressed in another.
3) Regime mapping is applied to zones first, then inherited conservatively by families.
4) Secondary zone membership should not receive the same posture strength as a zone's primary family seed.
5) Market-native support objects remain support/context only.
6) Primitive support materials do not gain family permission automatically.
7) NO_TRADE and UNKNOWN remain suppressive regimes by design.
8) Range-dirty and transition contexts must remain conflict-aware and conservatively permissioned.

============================================================
CURRENT CONSERVATIVE REGIME READ
============================================================
Trend regimes:
- TREND_CONTINUATION is the clearest primary fit.
- TREND_PULLBACK and breakout/continuation expansion labels remain subordinate or conditional.
- Range mean-reclaim and reversal labels stay constrained.

Range regimes:
- RANGE_MEAN_RECLAIM is the clearest primary fit for RANGE_BALANCED.
- RANGE_DIRTY remains structurally fragile and should not receive broad permission merely because several families can be narrated into it.

Compression / expansion regimes:
- COMPRESSION is the clearest home for compression-first edges.
- EXPANSION is the clearest home for breakout and continuation-expansion behavior.
- Compression and expansion overlap must stay explicit rather than collapsed into one permissive bucket.

Transition regimes:
- REVERSAL_EXHAUSTION is the clearest primary fit.
- Other families may remain conditional or weak-fit, but transition should not become a catch-all permission regime.

============================================================
ANTI-OVERPERMISSION RULES
============================================================
- not every zone should remain active in every regime
- too many conditional mappings in one regime is a conflict warning, not a sign of opportunity abundance
- primary posture should stay sparse
- secondary posture should remain narrower than narrative plausibility might suggest
- support objects, primitives, and contextual market-native records do not imply family licensing

============================================================
BOUNDARY NOTE
============================================================
E4 defines factory-level structural compatibility only.
It does not change runtime strategy logic, entry logic, exit logic, governor formulas, execution behavior, AI authority, or live trading behavior.
