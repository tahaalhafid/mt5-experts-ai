MATRIX RULES
- Minimum required inputs are the narrowest normal starting set.
- Optional inputs do not imply always-on retrieval.
- Forbidden inputs remain forbidden in every authority state.
- The matrix is subordinate to H6 authority state. If H6 is AI_OFF, no task execution is permitted.
- COUNCIL_CONTEXTUAL_ADVISORY remains contextual and non-directional only.
- BOUNDED_PROPOSAL_GENERATION remains reviewable-proposal-only and does not authorize auto-apply.
