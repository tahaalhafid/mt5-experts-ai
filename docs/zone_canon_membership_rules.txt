ZONE CANON MEMBERSHIP RULES
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory zone membership rules only.
Truth posture: Canon membership mapping only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
MEMBERSHIP ROLES
============================================================
- ZONE_PRIMARY_EDGE_FAMILY
  Main canonical family seed for the zone.

- ZONE_SECONDARY_EDGE_FAMILY
  Related family with meaningful overlap or relevance to the zone, but not treated as the zone's main seed.

- ZONE_SUPPORTING_PRIMITIVE
  Primitive, detector, filter, entry pattern, exit model, or support module that helps describe or support a zone without becoming family identity.

- ZONE_MARKET_NATIVE_SUPPORT_OBJECT
  Market-native raw context object linked to a zone as support, suppression, or context only.

- ZONE_RESEARCH_ONLY_REFERENCE
  A useful reference or candidate that should remain under review and not be treated as active canon driver.

- ZONE_LEGACY_REFERENCE
  Historical or downgraded material that remains attached for reference only and must not compete directly with active canon.

============================================================
MAPPING DISCIPLINE
============================================================
1) Core runtime family seeds may become zone-primary only where structure is clear.
2) Conditional families normally enter as zone-secondary unless there is exceptional reason to promote them.
3) Primitive materials do not receive family status.
4) Legacy library identities remain legacy/reference unless later phases explicitly re-evaluate them.
5) Market-native objects may support or suppress a zone, but do not become runtime families in E3.
6) A material may be attached secondarily to another zone when overlap is real, but primary home must stay explicit.
