policy_note=This policy defines severity tiers, investigation depth, cadence intent, cooldown/suppression discipline, and escalation rules only. It does not implement scheduling.

SEVERITY TIERS
- LIGHT
  Meaning=Routine visibility, explanation, or governance review.
- STANDARD
  Meaning=Material but non-critical incident, drift, or repeated operational pattern.
- HIGH
  Meaning=Strong incident needing structured investigation, likely root-cause work, or repeated quality/safety concern.
- CRITICAL
  Meaning=Safety, rollback, truth, diagnostics, or repeated execution issue with strong operator review need.

INVESTIGATION DEPTH TIERS
- SUMMARY_ONLY
  Meaning=ALWAYS_VISIBLE_SUMMARIES first; no raw retrieval unless explicitly escalated by policy.
- TARGETED_RETRIEVAL
  Meaning=Summaries first, then bounded ON_DEMAND_RETRIEVAL slices scoped by decision_id, position_id, deal_id, incident window, or bounded time range.
- DEEP_INVESTIGATION
  Meaning=Multiple bounded slices and cross-artifact reconstruction under stronger trigger conditions and daily-cap constraints. Never broad unbounded raw-all-data mode.

CADENCE INTENT
- PER_EVENT
- PER_CANDIDATE_EVENT
- HOURLY
- DAILY
- THREE_DAY
- WEEKLY
- ON_REQUEST
- ON_REVIEW
- PHASE_CHANGE
- NONE_BY_DEFAULT
