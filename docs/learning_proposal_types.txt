PROPOSAL FAMILIES

- OBSERVATION_ONLY_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Record a bounded learning observation that is review-relevant but not yet appropriate for change-oriented handling.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=LEARNING_EVIDENCE_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  expected_risk_class=LOW_RISK_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Explicit observation scope, limitation disclosure, and no implied change request.
  prohibited_usage=May not disguise a mutation request or broad optimization claim.

- INSTRUMENTATION_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Suggest bounded instrumentation or observability refinement for better governed understanding.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=LEARNING_EVIDENCE_BUNDLE,PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  expected_risk_class=LOW_RISK_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must name the exact instrumentation gap, expected review value, and validation intent.
  prohibited_usage=May not imply live strategy tuning or direct execution control.

- INVESTIGATION_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Request a bounded deeper review, replay, or root-cause investigation where evidence is meaningful but not yet sufficient for stronger action.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=LEARNING_EVIDENCE_BUNDLE,PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  expected_risk_class=MEDIUM_RISK_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must define investigation scope, unanswered question, and why current evidence is not enough.
  prohibited_usage=May not be framed as a hidden implementation request.

- EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Prepare a tightly bounded candidate for later controlled experiment consideration.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=HIGH_RISK_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must define experiment purpose, evidence basis, caution note, validation intent, and rollback note.
  prohibited_usage=May not authorize experiment launch or live enablement by itself.

- BOUNDED_POLICY_REVIEW_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Suggest a governed review of a specific learning-related policy boundary, handling rule, or review policy.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=GOVERNANCE_SENSITIVE_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must name the exact policy area, bounded concern, review rationale, and mutation prohibition note.
  prohibited_usage=May not request authority expansion or direct trading-control changes.

- BOUNDED_THRESHOLD_REVIEW_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Suggest review of a narrowly identified threshold or tolerance area as a governed subject for later human handling only.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=HIGH_RISK_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must remain threshold-review only, with exact scope, evidence support, validation intent, and rollback note.
  prohibited_usage=May not become a direct threshold mutation request.

- BOUNDED_GATING_REVIEW_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Suggest review of a narrowly bounded gating rule, readiness rule, or escalation-handling boundary.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=GOVERNANCE_SENSITIVE_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must specify gating scope, exact concern, expected governance value, and non-authoritative notice.
  prohibited_usage=May not directly enable, disable, block, or allow live behavior.

- LEARNING_SCOPE_TIGHTENING_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Recommend tighter learning scope, stricter evidence requirements, narrower output handling, or stronger governance discipline.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=GOVERNANCE_SENSITIVE_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must specify which learning scope is being tightened and why measured evidence justifies it.
  prohibited_usage=May not silently disable unrelated capabilities or alter live trading logic.

- LEARNING_SCOPE_NARROWING_PROPOSAL
  proposal_purpose=Recommend narrowing a task family, evidence family usage pattern, or advisory-learning coupling when usefulness is low or governance cost is high.
  allowed_evidence_bundle_types=PROPOSAL_SUPPORT_BUNDLE,EXPERIMENT_CANDIDATE_SUPPORT_BUNDLE
  direct_apply_allowed=false
  auto_apply_allowed=false
  required_review_level=REVIEW_BOARD
  expected_risk_class=GOVERNANCE_SENSITIVE_REVIEW
  boundedness_requirements=Must define the exact scope being narrowed, expected burden reduction, and limitation note.
  prohibited_usage=May not become a hidden rollback or live disablement instruction.

PROPOSAL PACKAGING TEMPLATE
Required fields:
- learning_proposal_id
- proposal_family_name
- proposal_scope
- target_domain
- evidence_bundle_references
- evidence_sufficiency_state
- evidence_quality_grade
- rationale
- expected_benefit
- expected_risk
- boundedness_note
- limitation_note
- required_review_level
- suggested_next_step
- validation_intent
- rollback_note
- direct_apply_allowed=false
- auto_apply_allowed=false
- non_authoritative_notice

Optional fields:
- affected_zone_or_family
- relevant_review_window
- related_repository_records
- related_proposal_history_refs

PACKAGING RULE
A proposal package must remain a review artifact only.
Any package that omits scope, evidence linkage, limitations, or non-authoritative notice is incomplete by definition.
