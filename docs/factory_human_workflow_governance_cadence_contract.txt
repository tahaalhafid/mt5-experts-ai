Purpose
- Define bounded human-operational governance structure for the factory.
- Clarify who is expected to review what, what checkpoint applies, and what cadence applies.
- Keep workflow/governance meaning explicit without creating workflow automation or runtime behavior.

What workflow/governance means here
- Human workflow expresses expected bounded human-operational ownership, review checkpoint category, review rhythm, and current workflow posture interpretation.
- Governance cadence expresses review rhythm expectation only.
- Workflow/governance may describe review responsibility and hold/archive/escalation posture.
- Workflow/governance does not create scheduling, automation, approval, validation, experiment success, canon eligibility, or runtime permission.

What workflow/governance must not imply
- runtime permission
- auto-promotion
- validation success
- experiment success
- canon eligibility
- trading value proof
- AI authority escalation
- workflow automation
- review completion unless explicitly emitted elsewhere

How workflow differs from other truths
- Workflow differs from lifecycle:
  lifecycle states describe where the material currently sits architecturally; workflow describes expected human-operational handling.
- Workflow differs from routing:
  routing describes bounded downstream destination/handoff; workflow describes who is expected to act and under what checkpoint/cadence/posture.
- Workflow differs from validation:
  workflow may require review without implying validation happened.
- Workflow differs from experiment success:
  experiment packaging review posture is not experiment approval or success.
- Workflow differs from canon eligibility:
  no workflow posture implies canon eligibility.

Interpretation discipline
- human_review_role means expected bounded reviewer category, not person assignment.
- governance_checkpoint means expected review checkpoint category, not completion.
- governance_cadence means expected review rhythm, not scheduler execution.
- workflow_posture means current workflow interpretation only, not approval or validation.

Current tracking model
- CURRENT_WORKFLOW_STATE_DERIVED_NOT_EVENT_HISTORY
- No append-only workflow history is created in this phase.
- No ticketing/workflow engine is created in this phase.

Boundary honesty
- non-runtime
- non-automatic
- non-authoritative
- human review required where workflow fields are emitted
- no direct registry commit implied
- no direct canon promotion implied
- no direct runtime use implied
