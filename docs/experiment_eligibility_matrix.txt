1. MAPPING RULE
This matrix maps L3 proposal families and risk classes to experiment classes for design consideration only.
No mapping in this document authorizes live activation.

4. NON-ELIGIBLE CASES
- REJECTED proposals
- DEFERRED proposals without re-entry decision
- proposals missing validation design
- proposals missing rollback readiness
- proposals with hidden live activation intent
- proposals with unsupported broad-scope claims
- proposals with unresolved ambiguity too high for the requested class
