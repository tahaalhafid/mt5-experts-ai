1) PURPOSE

This contract defines what utility means inside the factory.
Utility accounting exists to describe bounded downstream usefulness of factory structure and analytical work.

Utility accounting in this factory may express:
- contradiction exposure
- ambiguity exposure
- false precision exposure
- structural classification clarity
- routing clarity
- early rejection support
- review load reduction potential
- candidate packaging clarity
- explicit no-additional-downstream-utility states
- support-only / zero-emission utility truth

Utility accounting in this factory must NOT imply:
- review happened
- validation succeeded
- experiment succeeded
- canon eligibility
- runtime permission
- live trading value
- profitability
- real-world edge proof

2) DIFFERENCE FROM OTHER FACTORY LAYERS

Lifecycle truth answers:
- where a material currently is

Routing truth answers:
- where a material is currently routed next and why

Internal intelligence truth answers:
- what bounded analytical delta is claimed beyond decomposition

Utility truth answers:
- what bounded downstream usefulness is being claimed
- what type of practical structural/analytical value is present
- what no-impact state applies if no additional downstream utility is observable

Utility accounting does NOT replace lifecycle, routing, internal intelligence, review, or validation truth.

3) UTILITY CLAIM CATEGORIES

Defined categories:
- CONTRADICTION_EXPOSURE
- AMBIGUITY_EXPOSURE
- FALSE_PRECISION_EXPOSURE
- STRUCTURAL_CLASSIFICATION_CLARITY
- ROUTING_CLARITY
- EARLY_REJECTION_SUPPORT
- REVIEW_LOAD_REDUCTION_POTENTIAL
- CANDIDATE_PACKAGING_CLARITY
- NO_ADDITIONAL_DOWNSTREAM_UTILITY
- SUPPORT_ONLY_NO_EMITTED_UTILITY

These are utility categories only.
They are not proof of downstream success, review success, experiment success, canon value, or trading value.

4) NO-IMPACT / LOW-IMPACT STATES

Defined states:
- NO_IMPACT_DETECTED
- SUPPORT_ONLY_NO_EMISSION
- STRUCTURAL_ONLY_NO_DOWNSTREAM_EFFECT
- UTILITY_NOT_YET_OBSERVABLE
- INSUFFICIENT_DOWNSTREAM_EVIDENCE

These states are conservative.
They preserve zero-state honesty and prevent architectural richness from being mistaken for emitted usefulness.

5) UTILITY BASIS MODEL

Defined basis categories:
- MATERIAL_STRUCTURE_ONLY
- DECOMPOSITION_CLARITY
- NORMALIZED_RECORD_CLARITY
- INTERNAL_INTELLIGENCE_DELTA
- ROUTING_DISCIPLINE
- MIXED_FACTORY_DISCIPLINE
- ZERO_STATE_TRUTH_PRESERVATION

Every utility claim should be able to state its basis.
Utility basis must remain interpretable without guessing.

6) BLOCKER INTERPRETATION DISCIPLINE

Routing blockers may mean either:
- blocker to the current downstream route
or
- blocker to progression beyond the current downstream route

This phase does NOT redesign routing semantics.
For utility clarity only, the architecture may distinguish:
- current_route_blocker_interpretation
- onward_progression_blocker_interpretation

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, listed blockers on a material with an emitted current route are interpreted conservatively as blockers to progression beyond the emitted current route.

7) BOUNDARY RULES

Utility accounting remains:
- non-runtime
- non-mutative
- non-authoritative
- non-validating
- non-promotional
- non-executing

A utility claim may improve clarity or accountability.
It does not approve action by itself.

8) ZERO-STATE HONESTY

If emitted utility records are absent in a given layer:
- utility_supported may still be true
- utility_visibility_enabled may still be true
- utility_record_emitted must remain false
- a no-impact state such as SUPPORT_ONLY_NO_EMISSION or UTILITY_NOT_YET_OBSERVABLE must remain available

No utility record should be fabricated merely to populate the model.

9) CONTROL / DATA PLANE DISCIPLINE

This contract is control-plane truth.
Per-record utility claims are data-plane truth.
Mixed surfaces must state explicitly that they summarize rather than redefine record truth.

10) INTERPRETATION RULE

No utility claim may be interpreted as:
- proof of trading edge
- proof of profitability
- proof of runtime usefulness
- proof of canon worthiness
- proof of experiment success

Utility accounting is bounded architectural accountability only.
