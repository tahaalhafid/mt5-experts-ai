ZONE CANON COVERAGE MODEL
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory coverage model only.
Truth posture: Structural canon coverage only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
COVERAGE STATES
============================================================
- STRONG_COVERAGE
  At least one clear zone-primary family seed exists, with meaningful support/context around it.

- MODERATE_COVERAGE
  A zone is structurally represented, but either seed depth, support depth, or boundary clarity is still limited.

- SPARSE_COVERAGE
  A zone exists conceptually but is underseeded or thinly supported.

- OVERCROWDED_COVERAGE
  Too many similar labels compete inside a zone. This is a clutter warning, not a quality signal.

- OVERLAP_HEAVY
  A zone shares too much territory with adjacent zones and needs sharper future separation.

- COVERAGE_GAP_PRESENT
  A zone has visible structural weakness, missing seed depth, or over-reliance on primitives/placeholders.

============================================================
HOW TO READ COVERAGE
============================================================
Coverage does NOT mean:
- proven edge quality
- profitability
- runtime approval
- execution permission

Coverage means only:
- how well the current canon structure is populated and separated
