FACTORY EVIDENCE BINDING CONTRACT
version=PHASE7_FACTORY_EVIDENCE_BINDING_CONTRACT_V1

purpose
This contract defines how factory-side structural claims may be conservatively bound to downstream outcome truth without implying runtime value, validation, experiment success, canon eligibility, or trading proof.

what evidence binding means
Evidence binding is bounded traceability between an emitted factory-side record or claim and a downstream outcome reference already observable in factory-emitted truth surfaces.

what an evidence link is
An evidence link is a declared relationship between:
- a factory-side claim surface
- and a downstream outcome reference
with explicit linkage type, basis, limitations, and unobserved-state handling.

allowed expression
Evidence binding may express:
- which downstream outcome reference category is being used
- whether the link is direct, partial, indirect, supporting-only, or not yet linked
- what basis supports the link
- what downstream outcome remains unobserved
- what limitations apply
- what the binding must not imply

must not imply
Evidence binding must not imply:
- review approval
- validation success
- experiment success
- canon eligibility
- runtime permission
- profitability
- live trading value
- proof of real-world edge

difference from utility
Utility answers what bounded downstream usefulness is being claimed.
Evidence binding answers whether that claim or related architectural truth is traceably linked to a downstream outcome reference.

difference from review outcome
Evidence binding may reference a review-related downstream outcome category, but it does not mean review approval happened unless emitted review outcome truth exists.

difference from validation
Evidence binding is not validation.
A bound outcome reference is traceability, not validation.

difference from experiment success
Experiment packaging or review-related references are not experiment success proof.

difference from canon eligibility
A bound classification or archive outcome is not canon eligibility.

difference from live trading proof
No evidence binding field in this factory proves live trading value.

downstream outcome reference categories
- REVIEW_OUTCOME_REFERENCE
- GOVERNANCE_OUTCOME_REFERENCE
- EXPERIMENT_PACKAGING_OUTCOME_REFERENCE
- ARCHIVE_DISPOSITION_REFERENCE
- RESEARCH_ONLY_DISPOSITION_REFERENCE
- CLASSIFICATION_OUTCOME_REFERENCE
- NO_DOWNSTREAM_OUTCOME_REFERENCE

evidence linkage types
- DIRECT_LINK
- INDIRECT_LINK
- PARTIAL_LINK
- SUPPORTING_LINK_ONLY
- NOT_YET_LINKED
- NO_BINDABLE_OUTCOME
- ZERO_STATE_NO_BINDING

evidence binding basis model
- LIFECYCLE_TO_OUTCOME_BINDING
- ROUTING_TO_OUTCOME_BINDING
- UTILITY_TO_OUTCOME_BINDING
- INTERNAL_INTELLIGENCE_TO_REVIEW_BINDING
- CLASSIFICATION_TO_DISPOSITION_BINDING
- MIXED_FACTORY_TO_DOWNSTREAM_BINDING
- ZERO_STATE_TRUTH_ONLY

unbound or not-yet-observed states
- OUTCOME_NOT_YET_OBSERVED
- NO_BINDABLE_DOWNSTREAM_EVENT
- SUPPORT_ONLY_NO_OUTCOME_BINDING
- INSUFFICIENT_LINKAGE_EVIDENCE
- ZERO_STATE_NO_OUTCOME_BINDING

truth rule
Binding means traceability only.
Binding is not approval, validation, profitability, or runtime authority.
