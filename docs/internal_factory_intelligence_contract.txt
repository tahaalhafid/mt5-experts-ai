PURPOSE
This contract upgrades internal intelligence from a structurally correct snapshot surface into a more disciplined internal analytical artifact model.

INTERNAL INTELLIGENCE IS
- a non-authoritative internal analytical delta layer
- a bounded interpretation layer built on top of rich intake, decomposition, and normalized material structure
- a place where semantic clarification, codification interpretation, consistency exposure, hidden-assumption exposure, false-precision exposure, refinement hypothesis, and candidate packaging posture may be expressed

INTERNAL INTELLIGENCE IS NOT
- runtime control
- canon authority
- registry commit authority
- validation
- review outcome truth
- event history
- append-only replay
- experiment permission
- trading readiness

INTERNAL INTELLIGENCE RECORD
An internal intelligence record is a bounded analytical record that must answer:
- why this record exists
- what it adds beyond decomposition
- what ambiguity remains unresolved
- what review posture it carries
- what must not be inferred from it

DECOMPOSITION VS INTELLIGENCE
Decomposition expresses normalized structural interpretation of the material.
Internal intelligence expresses analytical delta beyond decomposition, such as:
- semantic clarification
- codification interpretation
- semantic-code consistency exposure
- hidden assumption exposure
- false precision exposure
- refinement hypothesis
- candidate packaging posture

If no meaningful analytical delta exists, that truth must remain explicit.

NON-AUTHORITATIVE BOUNDARIES
- semantic interpretation remains primary
- codification interpretation remains supplementary
- polished code must not override weak semantics
- no internal intelligence record implies runtime readiness
- no internal intelligence record implies canon eligibility
- no internal intelligence record implies registry commit
- human review remains required

CURRENT TRACKING MODE
- snapshot-oriented / current-state-oriented only
- not event history
- not review outcome history
- not workflow state history
