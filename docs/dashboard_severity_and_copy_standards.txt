SEVERITY=INFO
meaning=Bounded informational status with no current blocking implication.
copy_example=Architecture defined; rendering not active.

SEVERITY=NOTICE
meaning=Important bounded context or governance posture without immediate critical block.
copy_example=Pilot defined, not live.

SEVERITY=CAUTION
meaning=Partial, incomplete, zero-record, or mixed-plane condition that requires conservative interpretation.
copy_example=Source is partial; visible summary is incomplete.

SEVERITY=WARNING
meaning=Blocking, stale, or materially constrained posture that should remain visually clear.
copy_example=AI is off and readiness remains not ready.

SEVERITY=CRITICAL
meaning=Hard block or high-priority operational constraint.
copy_example=Runtime frozen; trading blocked.

COPY STANDARDS
- keep copy short
- keep copy specific
- do not dramatize
- do not market
- do not address investors
- do not imply approval or command authority
- prefer one bounded claim per line
