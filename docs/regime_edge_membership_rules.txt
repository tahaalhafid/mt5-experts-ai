REGIME-EDGE MEMBERSHIP RULES
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory regime membership rules only.
Truth posture: Structural compatibility mapping only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
REGIME POSTURE CLASSES
============================================================
- REGIME_ALLOWED_PRIMARY
  The zone/family is a clear structural fit for the regime and may later deserve primary licensing consideration.

- REGIME_ALLOWED_SECONDARY
  The zone/family remains structurally compatible, but is not treated as the regime's strongest first-choice fit.

- REGIME_CONDITIONAL
  The zone/family may be viable only when additional structural qualifiers are present.

- REGIME_WEAK_FIT
  The zone/family has limited structural compatibility and should not be treated as a strong regime home.

- REGIME_SUPPRESSED
  The zone/family should be treated as structurally inappropriate under the regime.

- REGIME_RESEARCH_ONLY
  The zone/family remains reference-only under the regime and should not be treated as a current licensing candidate.

============================================================
MAPPING DISCIPLINE
============================================================
1) Zones are mapped to regimes first.
2) Families inherit zone posture conservatively.
3) Zone-primary family seeds may inherit a zone posture directly.
4) Zone-secondary families normally receive one-step posture downgrade relative to the zone template.
5) Primitive materials do not receive family regime posture automatically.
6) Market-native support objects do not receive family regime posture automatically.
7) Legacy or research-only references must not be upgraded into allowed posture simply because they share a zone label.
