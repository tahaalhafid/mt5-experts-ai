policy_note=This matrix defines who normally reviews each learning proposal family, how escalation works, and which roles are prohibited from approval.

REVIEW ROLE BASELINE
- OPERATOR = may view and flag only; may not approve any learning proposal.
- ADMIN_REVIEWER = default triage role and first-line reviewer for lower-risk bounded proposals.
- REVIEW_BOARD = formal reviewer for higher-risk, structured, and governance-sensitive learning proposals.
- COMMITTEE_REVIEWER = exceptional reviewer for justified governance-sensitive escalations only.

FAMILY TO REVIEWER MAPPING

- OBSERVATION_ONLY_PROPOSAL
  primary_reviewer_role=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  optional_escalation_role=REVIEW_BOARD
  prohibited_reviewer_roles=OPERATOR
  rationale=Observation-only proposals are usually low-risk and often close, defer, or support later stronger packages rather than proceed directly.
  normal_decision_path=DEFER,REJECT,CLOSE_NO_ACTION,REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE

- INSTRUMENTATION_PROPOSAL
  primary_reviewer_role=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  optional_escalation_role=REVIEW_BOARD
  prohibited_reviewer_roles=OPERATOR
  rationale=Instrumentation proposals are commonly narrow and lower risk but still require governed review for scope and side effects.
  normal_decision_path=ACCEPT_FOR_GOVERNED_ACTION_PREPARATION,DEFER,REJECT,REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE

- INVESTIGATION_PROPOSAL
  primary_reviewer_role=ADMIN_REVIEWER
  optional_escalation_role=REVIEW_BOARD
  prohibited_reviewer_roles=OPERATOR
  rationale=Investigation proposals often need triage first and may escalate if uncertainty, cross-scope implications, or repeated unresolved patterns appear.
  normal_decision_path=REQUIRE_MORE_EVIDENCE,DEFER,ACCEPT_FOR_EXPERIMENT,ESCALATE,REJECT

ROLE PROHIBITIONS
- OPERATOR may not issue ACCEPT_FOR_EXPERIMENT.
- OPERATOR may not issue ACCEPT_FOR_GOVERNED_ACTION_PREPARATION.
- OPERATOR may not approve or promote any learning proposal.
- ADMIN_REVIEWER may not treat acceptance as runtime activation.
- REVIEW_BOARD may not convert a reviewed proposal into live mutation.
- COMMITTEE_REVIEWER may not create direct execution authority through review.

MAPPING PRINCIPLES
- Proposal family mapping must remain conservative and risk-aware.
- Governance-sensitive or higher-risk proposals default upward toward REVIEW_BOARD rather than downward toward fast approval.
- Committee involvement is exceptional, not routine.
- Weak proposals should not escalate merely to avoid rejection.
