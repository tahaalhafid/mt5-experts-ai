VISIBILITY CONSTRAINTS
- SHADOW_ONLY authority does not convert outputs into runtime-authoritative truth.
- OPERATOR_VISIBLE does not imply direct control authority.
- ADMIN_VISIBLE does not imply auto-apply or direct mutation authority.
- REVIEW_BOARD_VISIBLE is review-oriented only.
- COMMITTEE_VISIBLE is briefing/review-oriented only.
