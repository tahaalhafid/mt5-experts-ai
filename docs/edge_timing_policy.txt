============================================================
WHAT ENTRY TIMING MEANS
============================================================
Entry timing means:
- the factory's "why now" layer for an edge family
- the layer that explains whether an otherwise structurally compatible edge is early, ready, validated, incomplete, late, exhausted, failed, or not applicable
- the layer that separates family identity from immediate trigger timing

Timing is therefore distinct from:
- zone membership
- regime structural fit
- execution permission
- live runtime entry logic

Structural fit is necessary but not sufficient.
A family may be structurally compatible with a zone and regime while still being:
- too early
- not yet confirmed
- already too late
- already exhausted
- timing-invalid under current confirmation quality

============================================================
TIMING INTERPRETATION PRIMITIVES
============================================================
The factory now recognizes the following timing/trigger interpretation primitives:

- confirmation_quality
  Whether the confirming behavior is structurally persuasive or merely suggestive.

- close_quality
  Whether body-close behavior supports the thesis rather than leaving it wick-dominant or indecisive.

- wick_rejection_quality
  Whether rejection behavior is real structural rejection or only cosmetic intrabar noise.

- expansion_quality
  Whether expansion is decisive, directional, and contextually aligned rather than thin or unstable.

- acceptance_quality
  Whether price accepts beyond a break/reclaim rather than only printing a brief excursion.

- follow_through_quality
  Whether the move continues after confirmation or stalls immediately.

- incomplete_confirmation
  Structural plausibility is present, but the trigger remains underconfirmed.

- no_follow_through_deterioration
  Initial trigger appearance degrades because continuation/acceptance does not persist.

============================================================
ANTI-PREMATURE / ANTI-LATE DISCIPLINE
============================================================
The factory must explicitly distinguish:

- early but structurally plausible
  The edge thesis is coherent, but trigger evidence is still immature.

- ready and confirmed
  Timing state has matured from pre-trigger into validated trigger behavior.

- already too late
  Structural alignment may remain present, but asymmetry is degraded because the move has already traveled.

- already exhausted
  Structural and timing energy have deteriorated; fresh edge quality is materially reduced.

- structurally attractive but timing-invalid
  The market thesis may still look interesting, but trigger quality, follow-through, or timing freshness are insufficient.

No timing map may collapse these into one vague "valid" bucket.

============================================================
ANTI-BLOAT DISCIPLINE
============================================================
- Trigger archetypes are factory timing archetypes, not runtime strategy names.
- Not every family requires many archetypes at this phase.
- Generic confirmation language must be treated as a weakness, not as precision.
- Late-sensitive families must remain marked as late-sensitive rather than over-permissively "ready."
- Suppressed regime posture must map to timing non-applicability rather than trigger optimism.
