GLOBAL PROHIBITION RULE
- No learning artifact may mutate runtime directly.
- No learning artifact may overwrite active plan truth.
- No learning artifact may elevate AI authority.
- No learning artifact may create a hidden or silent control path.

PROHIBITED ACTOR BEHAVIORS
- AI may not self-authorize a learning conclusion.
- AI may not treat proposal acceptance as deployment authority.
- AI may not bypass A7 review workflow.
- AI may not bypass A8 effectiveness review when later learning usefulness must be assessed.
- Human reviewers may not interpret L1 as granting live mutation authority.

SAFE INTERPRETATION RULE
- If a future learning idea appears useful but creates a direct mutation path, it remains prohibited under L1 until separately re-governed in a later approved phase.
- Ambiguity must be resolved conservatively in favor of bounded, review-only handling.

ENFORCEMENT INTENT
- This policy is normative and governance-defining.
- L1 does not implement runtime blockers, but it does define what later phases must never violate without explicit re-governance.
