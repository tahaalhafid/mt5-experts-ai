BOUNDARY PRINCIPLES
- Learning scope is indirect, review-governed, and non-autonomous.
- Learning is allowed to improve institutional understanding and governed change planning.
- Learning is not allowed to create silent authority, hidden mutation, or live optimization loops.
- No learning boundary in this document modifies trading behavior.

WHAT LEARNING MAY INFLUENCE INDIRECTLY
- evidence_quality_for_review
- proposal_quality_for_review
- experiment_candidate_selection
- prioritization_of_future_investigation
- governed_understanding_of_patterns_and_failures
- review_board_context_for_bounded_change_consideration
- impact_audit_quality
- future_scope_tightening_or_narrowing_decisions

WHAT LEARNING MAY NOT INFLUENCE DIRECTLY
- live_strategy_logic
- live_entry_logic
- live_exit_logic
- live_governor_formulas
- live_failure_detector_formulas
- live_execution_quality_formulas
- dirty_environment_logic
- trend_continuation_reinforcement_logic
- live_risk_thresholds
- live_runtime_weights
- direct_ai_authority_state
- direct_trade_permission
- direct_buy_sell_direction
- direct_order_execution_behavior
- truth_source_overwrite

GOVERNED LEARNING BOUNDARIES
boundary_class=EVIDENCE_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Learning may collect and structure evidence only from allowed inputs and review-governed artifacts.

boundary_class=ANALYSIS_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Learning analysis may identify patterns, anomalies, drift, or root-cause hypotheses, but analysis does not itself become a mutative command.

boundary_class=PROPOSAL_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Learning may produce bounded proposals or experiment candidates only as review objects under A7 governance.

boundary_class=REVIEW_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Learning-related outputs enter review and may be accepted, deferred, rejected, escalated, or closed without action. Review acceptance is not runtime activation.

boundary_class=PLANNING_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Controlled change planning may be prepared from accepted learning outputs, but planning remains distinct from runtime mutation and must be separately governed later.

boundary_class=VALIDATION_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Replay, validation, and impact review may strengthen or weaken learning conclusions, but do not self-authorize deployment.

boundary_class=AUDIT_BOUNDARY
boundary_rule=Learning usefulness, burden, and governance compliance must remain subject to A8 effectiveness review.

SELF-LEARNING TERMINOLOGY CLARIFICATION
- In this project, the phrase self-learning means a governed institutional loop that studies evidence and supports future review.
- It does not mean autonomous runtime adaptation.
- It does not mean self-modifying thresholds, formulas, strategies, or live execution behavior.
- It does not mean direct AI optimization of trading logic.

DECISION HANDOFF RULE
- Learning outputs may hand off into review-governed recommendations, experiment candidates, or bounded proposals.
- That handoff does not create direct mutation rights.
- Any downstream change remains outside L1 and must be explicitly governed in later phases.

TRACEABILITY EXPECTATION
- Learning artifacts should remain linkable to evidence sources, review decisions, validation context, and impact findings conceptually.
- L1 defines the boundary requirement, not the runtime storage engine.
