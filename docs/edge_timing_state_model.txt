EDGE TIMING STATE MODEL
Authority: Institutional Edge Factory timing-state model only.
Truth posture: Timing-state interpretation only. No runtime behavior change.

============================================================
TIMING STATES
============================================================
- PRE_TRIGGER
  Structural setup is plausible, but timing trigger conditions are not yet mature.

- TRIGGER_READY
  Timing is approaching validity and key conditions are aligning, but full validation is not yet claimed.

- TRIGGER_VALIDATED
  Timing conditions are sufficiently aligned for the factory to treat the trigger as structurally valid.

- TRIGGER_INCOMPLETE_CONFIRMATION
  The thesis may be plausible, but confirmation quality remains incomplete.

- TRIGGER_LATE
  The trigger may have been valid earlier, but timing freshness is now degraded.

- TRIGGER_EXHAUSTED
  The move or setup has already spent most of its fresh edge energy.

- TRIGGER_FAILED
  The expected trigger progression broke down materially.

- TRIGGER_RECLAIM_VALID
  Reclaim/return behavior is the specific validation mode.

- TRIGGER_POST_SWEEP_VALID
  Post-sweep behavior is the specific validation mode.

- TRIGGER_BREAK_ACCEPT_VALID
  Break-and-accept behavior is the specific validation mode.

- TRIGGER_NOT_APPLICABLE
  Under the current regime posture, timing should not be interpreted as currently applicable.

============================================================
STATE DISCIPLINE
============================================================
- PRE_TRIGGER is not TRIGGER_READY.
- TRIGGER_READY is not TRIGGER_VALIDATED.
- TRIGGER_VALIDATED is not execution permission.
- TRIGGER_LATE and TRIGGER_EXHAUSTED must remain explicit and may coexist with otherwise attractive structure.
- TRIGGER_NOT_APPLICABLE overrides familiar visual patterns when regime posture is structurally suppressive.

============================================================
AMBIGUITY DISCIPLINE
============================================================
When timing specificity is weak, the factory should prefer:
- PRE_TRIGGER
- TRIGGER_INCOMPLETE_CONFIRMATION
- TRIGGER_NOT_APPLICABLE

instead of prematurely claiming:
- TRIGGER_VALIDATED

This applies especially to:
- generic continuation language
- breakout language without acceptance quality
- micro-expansion patterns without follow-through
- reversal language without reclaim/rejection quality
