============================================================
WHAT INVALIDATION MEANS
============================================================
Invalidation means:
- the point at which the edge thesis is no longer structurally credible
- the point at which reclaim, acceptance, continuation, release, or exhaustion logic materially breaks down
- a structural thesis-failure concept, not a generic discomfort concept

Invalidation is NOT:
- every adverse tick
- every temporary wobble
- every noisy excursion
- "price moved against us a little"
- a substitute for later execution or exit policy

A valid invalidation concept should answer:
- what part of the thesis failed
- why that failure matters structurally
- why ordinary noise should NOT be confused with thesis failure

============================================================
INVALIDATION VS NOISE
============================================================
Temporary noise may include:
- small back-and-fill inside still-valid structure
- wick noise that does not break the defended logic
- incomplete follow-through that has not yet broken the core thesis
- local hesitation before a clean accept/reclaim structure is resolved

Invalidation becomes more credible when:
- reclaim is materially lost
- continuation hold/reset materially fails
- break acceptance materially fails
- release fails and structure returns into the prior premise
- exhaustion/reversal thesis collapses into renewed initiative against it

============================================================
FAMILY-SPECIFIC INVALIDATION READ
============================================================
- LIQUIDITY_REVERSAL
  Emphasize:
  - sweep/reclaim thesis failure
  - reclaim-loss
  - reversal collapse back into initiative

- MEAN_RECLAIM
  Emphasize:
  - edge rejection failure
  - reclaim loss
  - failed return into the rotation path

- TREND_CONTINUATION
  Emphasize:
  - continuation hold failure
  - structure reset failure
  - no-follow-through deterioration after a supposed continuation trigger

- TREND_PULLBACK_CONTINUATION
  Emphasize:
  - pullback hold failure
  - pullback acceptance failure
  - continuation not actually resuming after the hold thesis

- COMPRESSION_BREAKOUT
  Emphasize:
  - failed release
  - failed acceptance after release
  - expansion promise collapsing back into compression body

- VOL_BREAKOUT
  Emphasize:
  - breakout acceptance failure
  - weak expansion becoming structurally non-credible
  - breakout crowding into nearby opposing liquidity

- EXPANSION_CONTINUATION
  Emphasize:
  - post-displacement reentry failure
  - no reacceptance after the displacement
  - continuation thesis losing room and edge freshness

- MICRO_RANGE_BREAK
  Emphasize:
  - micro release failure
  - failed micro acceptance
  - local structure failing too quickly to support real expansion

============================================================
ANTI-FALSE-PRECISION DISCIPLINE
============================================================
- Invalidation should not be over-precisely defined when thesis structure is still ambiguous.
- Geometry quality should not be faked when target clarity is poor.
- A visually attractive trigger does not rescue bad asymmetry.
- One neat entry candle does not rescue crowded geometry.
- Poor geometry must remain visible even when timing looks attractive.
- Where the factory cannot honestly distinguish noise from thesis failure, ambiguity must remain explicit.
